# Hospital-Analytics-Dashboard

I’m excited to share my **Hospital Analysis Dashboard**, an interactive **Power BI project** designed to analyze hospital operations, patient data, and financial performance in a single unified view.

This dashboard helps hospital management and stakeholders quickly understand important insights such as patient flow, doctor performance, hospital resources, and revenue trends.

🔹 **Overview Dashboard**
Provides a complete snapshot of hospital performance including **total patients, bill count, doctor count, and staff count**. It also highlights **patient discharge trends, stock vs availability insights, and overall hospital activity**, helping management monitor performance at a glance.

🔹 **Patient Dashboard**
Focuses on patient-related insights such as **patient age category distribution, test results, and treatment status**. It also shows **patient medical tests, notes, and results**, allowing quick monitoring of patient health data and treatment outcomes.

🔹 **Doctor Dashboard**
Displays important doctor information including **doctor availability, specialization, department, salary, and ratings**. It also tracks **doctor commission, patient spending, and estimated earnings**, helping understand doctor performance and contribution to hospital revenue.

🔹 **Hospital Dashboard**
Shows operational insights such as **surgery schedules, patient appointments, and department activities**. This section helps track hospital workload and manage daily hospital operations more efficiently.

🔹 **Finance Dashboard**
Provides financial insights including **patient billing amount, hospital charges by category (surgery, rooms, tests, medicines), and commission calculations**. These insights help monitor hospital revenue and cost distribution.

-> **Key KPIs Included**
• Patient Count
• Bill Count
• Doctor & Staff Count
• Patient Discharge Rate
• Total Patient Spend
• Doctor Commission Earnings
• Appointment & Surgery Tracking

-> **Tools & Technologies Used**
Power BI | DAX | Data Modeling | Data Visualization

This project demonstrates how **data analytics and visualization can transform complex hospital data into clear insights that support better operational and financial decision-making.**
